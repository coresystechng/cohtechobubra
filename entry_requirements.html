Entry Requirements

Find out what is required of you before enrolling for any of the courses we offer at COHTECH Obubra.

  2. Entry Requirements
S/N Course Entry Requirements Duration
1 Diploma in Community Health
(CHEW)		 WASCE, NECO,GCE, NABTEB at O'level with (5) credits in English Language, Mathematics, Chemistry, Biology and Physics.

Junior Community Extension Workers (JCHEW) with 2 years’ post qualification experience in addition to the above requirement mentioned, and applicant MUST be registered with the Community Health Practitioners Registration Board of Nigeria (CHPRBN) and posses a valid practice license. 		3 years
2 Certificate in Community Health
(JCHEW)		 5 credit passes in WASCE, NECO, NABTEB Examination in English, Mathematics, Biology and any other two subjects at not more than two sittings. 2 years
3 Diploma FOR Health Technicians
(DIRECT ENTRY)		 5 O/L credit at not more than two (2) sittings in GCE/SSCE, NECO OR WAEC in English Lang, Mathematics, Health Science, Physics, Chemistry and Biology.
Admission is through JAMB UTME.		 4 years
4 Diploma for Pharmacy Technicians 5 credit passes in English, Mathematics, Biology ,Physics and Chemistry in any of GCE, SSCE, WAEC OR NECO in not more than 2 sittings. 3 years
5 Diploma For Medical Image Processing/X-ray Technicians 5 O/L credit at not more than two (2) sittings in GCE/SSCE, NECO OR WAEC in English Lang, Mathematics, Health Science, Physics, Chemistry and Biology. 3 years
6 Diploma In Environment Health Technology 5 O/L credit at not more than two (2) sittings in GCE/SSCE, NECO OR WAEC in English Lang, Mathematics, Health Science, Physics, Chemistry and Biology. 3 years
7 Diploma In Health Information Management 5 O/L credit at not more than two (2) sittings in GCE/SSCE, NECO OR WAEC in English Lang, Mathematics, Health Science, Physics, Chemistry and Biology. 3 years
8 Diploma for Laboratory Technicians 5 O/L credit passes (NECO or WAEC) in English Lang., Mathematics, Biology, Physics and Chemistry at not more than 2 sittings. 3 years
