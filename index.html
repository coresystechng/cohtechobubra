Welcome to COHTECH Obubra

The College of Health Technology, Obubra, Cross River State is a leading institution dedicated to training skilled professionals in the healthcare sector, who will contribute to the improvement of public health in Nigeria and beyond. With a commitment to excellence in education, research, and practical training, we equip our students with the knowledge and hands-on experience needed to excel in the ever-evolving healthcare sector. Our commitment to excellence ensures that graduates are well-prepared to contribute meaningfully to public health and community well-being.

Community Health

Community health professionals work to address social, economic, and environmental factors that impact health, aiming to create healthier communities through education, outreach, and intervention programs.

View Course

Medical Laboratory Technician

A Medical Laboratory Technician (MLT) is a healthcare professional trained to perform laboratory tests. They play a crucial role in healthcare by ensuring accurate test results that guide medical decisions and patient care.

View Course

Pharmacy Technician

A Pharmacy Technician is a healthcare professional who works under the supervision of a licensed pharmacist to assist in dispensing medications, managing prescriptions, and providing essential support in a pharmacy.
