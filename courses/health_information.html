HEALTH INFORMATION MANAGEMENT

Health Information Management Course

The need for a National Health information system that will supply accurate Health demographic data for the planning, administration, monitoring and evaluation of Health services, both local and national levels has been highlighted in the National Health Policy and Strategy oL1988. Health services planning and administration law suffered serious set back in the country owning to lack of adequate data. There is therefore, the need for trained personnel that will assist the organisation of Medical Records services at all levels of health care deliveries.

The success of the national information system depends to a large extent on the ability to collect adequate and reliable data at the local government level, which are organised and forward to the state and Federal level. There had brought about the need to a middle level manpower to function at the Local Government Levels. The name of programme was changed to Him in February 2005.

In April 2001 Pharmacist council of Nigeria granted accreditation to the school to commence the programme. Entrance examination was conducted in September 2001 while the 2002, 2004 set of Pharmacy Technicians resumed academic Work in January 2002. The school was granted full accreditation for the programme in August 2002. Upon completion of the course the Pharmacist Council of Nigeria (PCN) would issue Pharmacy Technician Certificate to successful trainees.

Training Objectives

The objectives of the programme are to produce a cadre of manpower that will among others perform the following functions at the Local Government Level for the successful implementation of the Primary Health Care Services. They are:

  • Organise and manage health information services in health care delivery system.
  • Organise and control health records.
  • Co-ordinate the collection, storage retrieval and insurance of health data.
  • Organise the preservation of health records.
  • Formulate, collate analyse, present and interprets health statistical data.
  • Plan a health records Departments effective patient flow.
  • Plan and manage a surveillance system for specified conditions.
  • Plan and manage on monitoring and evaluation (M and E) unit.
  • Participate in the conduct of research in health care system.
  • Standardize health records forms, designs and control.
  • Apply computer system to health care formation.
  • Advise on confidentiality, release of information from and preservation of records.
