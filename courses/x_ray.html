X-RAY TECHNICIAN

Description

The X-Ray Technician course is a subset of the Radiology department and is mostly taken independently as a Diploma course. The program covers both clinical and administrative skills along with hands-on training in radiology and laboratory tests.

Training Objectives

Students will be trained to:

  • Use X-Ray equipment to create images of a patient’s internal anatomy.
  • Assist doctors in diagnosing illnesses and injuries.
  • Comply with safety regulations for handling X-Ray equipment.

Admission Requirements

Five (5) credit passes in:

  • NECO/WAEC, including English Language, Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry, and Physics at not more than two sittings.
  • Duration: The course duration is 3 years (Six Semesters).
